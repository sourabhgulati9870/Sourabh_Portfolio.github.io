#SUN

The Sun is the star made of gases like hydrogen and helium. It is hot and has fire because of the reactions which take place on it’s surface. The planets revolve around the Sun because of the gravitational force of the Sun, thus forming the solar system. Sun provides us with sunlight and solar energy. We can use solar energy to produce eleçtricity. Sun is crucial for the survival of life on earth. In many cultures around the world, sun is treated as God. Everyone prefers a bright sunny day as it allows us to participate in any outdoor activity like a long drive or excursion. As the earth rotates, one hemisphere is exposed to sun leading to day while the other experiences night and hence the phenomenon of day and night occurs. Although, sun has various advantages,it also has drawback of UV rays which can harm us.
